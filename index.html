"Bridging the energy demand gap in Africa through Biogas"

Our mission at PBES involves turning biogas into an affordable and accessible source of clean energy for African communities. There have been major developments but equally there still remains a large gap in the energy demand whether due to economic limitations, geological positional restrictions or many other factors.

As of January 2025, there are reportedly still as much as 600 million Africans living in energy poverty as per the AFDB with an aim to half the figure by the year 2030.

We are committed to making a positive impact on the environment and the communities we serve.

What We Do

We produce biogas on a smaller scale by utilizing a modular system.

We collaborate with like-minded B2B partners and leverage complementary business models to scale up biogas production significantly.

We transform the slurry from our biogas system into green manure, which we refine into biofertilizer through additional processing.

Projects

Mini-Prototype

In early xxx we were able to put the theory to test by developing a small-scale Mini-Prototype using customary tooling & sealing procedures on a 50L PVC Container.
The experiment was a success as we collected a very small but viable amount of Biogas from our digester set-up over a retention time of 10 days.

Status: COMPLETE

MVP

The MVP is our initial full-scale Biogas Digester that is currently undergoing construction. The digester will have a volume of 24m3 and will predominantly run off of Cow Dung as a sole substrate before further co-digestion considerations. The digester will initially be used to supply electricity and lighting to the plant and site facilities before making the Biogas available on a full-scale for consumers.

Status: ONGOING

Main Plant

Looking into the near future, we aim to grow our project simutaenously while scaling up to a Main Plant of 100m3 to meet our site and consumer demands accordingly.
